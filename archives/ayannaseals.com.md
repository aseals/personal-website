## About

Ayanna Seals is a researcher and designer who is passionate about creating solutions within the fields of health, wellness, and civic technology. She is currently a Ph.D. candidate at NYU, previously receiving her B.S. in Communication Media from North Carolina State University and her M.S. in Integrated Digital Media from NYU.

As a Technology Consultant at OneSource she develops and integrates methodologies to enable small businesses to thrive while providing resources and support to aspiring entrepreneurs. She has done work for various companies and institutions, including OneSource, New York University, North Carolina State University, Bloomberg Associates, and News Corp.

## Research Interests

human computer interaction, augmented reality, self-focused attention, self-regulation, behavior change

## Projects

### UbiQomix: Human-Computer Interaction for Personal Omics
- **Tags:** HCI, Information Design, Health Information
- **Description:** Funded by the NSF, and in collaboration with Open Humans, we investigate how to design effective interaction techniques to empower nonexpert users to engage with and make sense of their personal omic information.
- **Link:** http://cs.wellesley.edu/~hcilab/pghci.html

### SONYC: Sounds of New York City
- **Tags:** HCI, Citizen Science/Participation, Public Health
- **Description:** Sonyc is a novel cyberphysical system that consists of a hybrid, distributed network of sensors deployed in the NYC that makes use of cutting-edge machine listening methods to constantly provide a rich description of their acoustic environment. My team is researching and developing the citizen science and 311 reporting application.
- **Link:** https://wp.nyu.edu/sonyc/

### Transforming Disclosures (Retirement Savings)
- **Tags:** HCI, Information Design, Financial Education
- **Description:** This project explores informative interactive design extensions for online retirement savings platforms and their effects on consumer behavior and decision making.

### Decentra Apps
- **Tags:** Affordable Housing, P2P, Online Collaboration
- **Description:** Decentra Apps are a collection of web applications that enable and increase the scalability of peer-to-peer owned and managed affordable housing.

### (Commented out on old site) Sensory Steps
- **Tags:** Assistive Technology, Kinect Game
- **Description:** A Kinect game to bring physical and occupational therapy to life for children with hearing impairment, increasing their motivation and engagement.
